Our Mission

Our mission is centered on three strategic pillars to deliver reliable, sustainable energy to Northern India:

Fuel & Tech: Develop CO₂-fueled, carbon-neutral generators for reliable, 24x7 power generation.
Integration: Establish grid-tied microgrids across Northern India to ensure community resilience and support the main power grid.
Impact: Deliver uninterrupted power while permanently diverting industrial carbon emissions from the atmosphere.

Engineering the Carbon-Neutral Energy Generator

Revolutionary technology that use carbon dioxide as a fuel to generate clean electricity, creating a sustainable energy cycle that benefits both industry and environment.

How It Works

1

Fuel Processing

Liquid CO₂ is compressed and heated to desirable pressure and temperature.

2

Closed-Loop Power Conversion

The transcritical CO₂ is expanded in a bladeless trubine to rotate the shaft of the alternator.

3

Clean Energy Output

Generated electricity powers facilities while reducing atmospheric emissions

Key Benefits

Carbon Neutral Energy

Every kilowatt generated is CO₂ free, using captured industrial waste as fuel for a net-neutral energy production cycle.

Bi-Directional Financial Advantage

Achieves substantial cost savings and generates new revenue by selling surplus, clean power back to the utility grid.

Scalable Technology

Provides flexible, decentralized deployment, easily scaling from small facilities to large complexes and microgrids.
